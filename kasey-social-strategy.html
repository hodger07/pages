At 27, "Partner" is a power title but needs context or it sounds inflated. The experience section should show progression — he didn't get handed a partnership, he grew into it. "Entrepreneur" — most overused, least trusted title for men under 30. Maya: "Instant credibility hit."

Inspirational quotes — Claire: "The moment I see 'the grind never stops' I close the tab."

"Passionate about..." — everyone says this. It's filler.

The cockpit selfie as headshot — save for Instagram. On LinkedIn it reads as "look at me" not "I'm competent."

Revenue figures or dollar amounts — "multi-million" should be implied by the operation size, not stated.

Anything about grief, ALS, or personal loss — LinkedIn is not the place. That depth comes in person.

Every field filled perfectly — Claire: "A profile with every field completed at 27 is itself a red flag for manufactured."
