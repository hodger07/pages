What this means

Systems vs. Freedom: Birkman measures how he builds (rigid). PI measures how he responds to external structure (very flexible). These aren't the same thing — they describe the same man in two different contexts. He builds the tightest systems of anyone. Everyone else's systems are intolerable. That's not a contradiction to fix — it's the operating reality to work with.

Need vs. Withdrawal: SC Need 79 means he requires genuine respect and appreciation to function well. People Positive conditional means he only extends warmth when conditions are already right. The person in the room who needs connection most is also the one least likely to initiate it when it matters. He waits for the room to make it safe. The room waits for him to open. This is the wound in behavioral data.

Opus 4.6 Analysis — Longitudinal Review
The architecture hasn't changed. The cost of running System A has.

At 39, System A was sustainable. At 57 — with Jessica, with the losses, with finite time — the cost structure changed even if the wiring didn't. System B isn't about becoming someone different. It's about running the same architecture with a fuel source that matches where he actually is now.

The Creative Pattern is the most underexamined finding.

Creative DiSC: independent conclusions, logic-first, challenges status quo, best under difficulty, doesn't naturally explain reasoning, resists being grouped. This is the shape of his output for 17 years — how he built the business, managed the franchise, handled the software project, operates in coaching. He innovates alone. He concludes fast without narrating the path.

Low i (2008) + Persuasive 96th percentile (2019) = the warmth is real, not aspirational.

DiSC 2008: Influence at Segment 1 — near floor. Birkman 2019: Persuasive interests at 96th percentile. He deeply values connection and people-motivation but doesn't default to those tools. Leads with logic and challenge. When stressed, the warmth goes away entirely. System B's People Positive isn't constructing something new — it's activating a capacity that's been there the whole time but has been conditional.

The DC profile gave System A its tools. System A didn't create the DC profile.

The wound predates the profile by decades — but the profile was already locked in by 39. Five assessments across two decades through franchise betrayal, loss, ALS, sobriety, major business decisions. The wiring didn't move. Whatever happened to him didn't change who he is. The environment changed dramatically. The architecture didn't.
